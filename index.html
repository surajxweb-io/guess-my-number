How to play? 🎮

  • There is a random number genrated by the computer between 1 and 20, including both ends.
  • You need to make a guess in the space provided and lock it using the key.
  • If the computer generated number is higher than the number you guessed, it tells you - too low; and if your guessed number is higher than the computer generated number, it says two high .
  • You have 5 chances to make the guess right after which you lose the game.
  • You can always press, play again to, well, play again.
